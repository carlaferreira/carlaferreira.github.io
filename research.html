Research

Interests

  • Models of concurrency and distribution — design models, languages, and tools to express and reason about concurrent and distributed systems, addressing aspects as data replication.
  • Foundations of cloud computing — design of programming models that capture the abstractions of large-scale distributed systems.
  • Language-based techniques for low-code software development.
